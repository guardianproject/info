**CuttingRoom and ProofMode Team Up for Content Authenticity Support on iPhones**

In today's digital landscape, where increasingly "AI Slop" runs rampant, the integrity of media has never been more critical. It's a constant battle to discern fact from fiction, and the stakes are high. That's why we're thrilled to announce a partnership that aims to arm journalists and content creators with a powerful weapon in this fight: the integration of the Coalition for Content Provenance and Authentication (C2PA) standard into the CuttingRoom Reporter mobile app for iOS.

Think about it -- how many times have you seen a shocking video or image online and wondered, "Is this real?". The generative AI image and video tools from OpenAI, Google, Meta, Runway and others are increasingly realistic with basic prompts. This uncertainty erodes trust in what people see, and creates fertile ground for manipulation. CuttingRoom, a trusted tool for mobile journalism, is partnering with us to tackle this problem head-on.

By embedding our SimpleC2PA software development kit directly into the CuttingRoom Reporter app, journalists can now cryptographically sign and timestamp their captured media right from their smartphones. This creates an unbreakable chain of evidence, as close to "glass to glass" as possible, proving that the content hasn't been tampered with since capture.

In an era where anyone can publish anything, the ability to verify the source and integrity of media is crucial. With C2PA, journalists can stand behind their work with confidence, knowing that their content is tamper-proof. For newsroom editors, and viewers, it's about making informed decisions. When you see a C2PA-verified video or image, you can trust that it hasn't been manipulated. This empowers you to engage with media critically and discern fact from fiction.

In a world where misinformation can have devastating consequences, this partnership is a significant step forward. We're not just building an app; we're building a foundation for a more transparent and trustworthy media landscape, from journalist to viewer.

The updated CuttingRoom Reporter app with ProofMode C2PA features is available on the Apple App Store now. We believe this is a game-changer for mobile journalism, and we can't wait to see the impact it has on the way we consume and share media.

## About ProofMode

ProofMode is an open-source project developed in collaboration by Guardian Project, Okthanks, and WITNESS, with support of the Filecoin Foundation for the Decentralized Web and the Knight Foundation. It captures, authenticates, and verifies smartphone multimedia from source to recipient, empowering activists, journalists, and everyday people. For more information, visit [proofmode.org](https://proofmode.org).
