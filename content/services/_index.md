## What we can do for you

- [Development Services](/services/development/): We offer services for-hire for a variety of work related to our mission, projects and code-base.
 
- [Digital Security Advisory and Training Services](/services/digital-security/): We take a holistic, people-first approach to digital security assessments, analyzing not just your technology, but also the processes, policies, and individuals that keep your organization and the communities you serve safe. 

- [Holistic Tech Assessments](https://unicef.github.io/holistic-assessments-for-ict4d/): With UNICEF, we co-developed a new process for engaging with technology within the Information and Communications Technology for Development (ICT4D) space. In particular, the work focuses on the benefits and impacts of adopting free and open-source software that is secure and privacy-focused.
