People, Apps and Code You Can Trust

Founded in 2009, Guardian Project is a global collective of designers, trainers, researchers, and developers building open software to enhance privacy and security for all. Our mission is to push the boundaries of mobile and privacy-enhancing technologies. Through this, we work to give people a voice, enhance safety, ensure authenticity, and provide access to knowledge regardless of location or connectivity.

Partners and Collaborators

Apps We Endorse

To achieve our goal of a comprehensive, privacy- and security-focused communications solution, Guardian is driven both by internal development and the open-source community at large. In cases where a viable, vetted, and usable product already fills the communications needs of our target audience, we will recommend apps that work.

Our Work

Whether you are an average person looking to affirm your rights or an activist, journalist or humanitarian organization looking to safeguard your work, we can help address the threats you face. Below are projects we have led, incubated, or actively contribute to and support.

Orbot

A Tor-powered VPN for Android and iOS that keeps app traffic safe and unblocked, through our 15+ year partnership with the Tor Project.

Learn More

Proofmode

We believe in a future where every camera will have a Proof Mode that can be enabled and every viewer an ability to verify-then-trust what they are seeing.

Learn More

Convene

A Matrix-based chat platform for secure and private communication and sharing, co-designed with human rights advocates and digital security trainers.

Learn More

ButterBox

An off-grid, delay-tolerant communication and sharing platform, providing connectivity and services when the internet is not available or not safe.

Learn More

Onion Browser

A free, open-source Tor-powered web browser for iOS that helps you access the internet with more safety and privacy.

Learn More

F-Droid

An installable catalogue of free and open source software for Android. We are longtime contributors to the platform and host our own app repository.

Learn More

Circulo

Helps you stay securely connected to a circle of support. Establish safety and check-in protocols, send alerts, and share your location.

Learn More

Clean Insights

A secure, private measurement platform focused on answering key questions about app usage patterns without invasive surveillance.

Learn More

CDR Link

A secure helpdesk providing Signal, PGP, WhatsApp and Telegram channels for your community, the gold standard for human rights helpdesks.

Learn More
